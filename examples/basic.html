Etmodel plugin example

If the population of the Netherlands grew to million people, then the total energy cost would increase by % (from to billion Euros).

The Netherlands has a lot of natural gas reserves. Unfortunately they are running out, so that if we didn't switch to different sources of energy (coal, oil, solar, wind, etc) by the year of 2050 % of our energy has to be imported. This is problematic, as the Netherlands has no control on gas prices. A gas price increase by % it can have dramatic effects to the total cost of energy.
